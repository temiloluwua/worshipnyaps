Privacy Policy

Worship and Yapps · Last updated May 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Worship and Yapps ("we", "us", or "the app") collects, uses, and protects information you provide when you use our iOS app and website at worshipnyaps.com.

1. Information we collect

We collect only what is necessary to operate the app:

  • Account information — name, email address, and (if you choose phone sign-in) phone number, used to create and secure your account.
  • Profile content — profile photo, cover photo, bio, spiritual gifts, and any other information you choose to add to your profile.
  • User-generated content — topics, comments, event posts, RSVPs, direct messages, and group messages you create within the app.
  • Connections — the list of users you connect with or follow.
  • Approximate location — only when you grant permission, and only to display nearby events on the map. Your precise location is never stored.
  • Photos — only when you choose to upload them as a profile picture, cover image, or event photo.
  • Authentication data from third parties — if you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive your name and email address from that provider only.

2. How we use your information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To display your profile, posts, and events to other users in accordance with your visibility settings.
  • To deliver direct and group messages.
  • To show nearby events on the map.
  • To send security verification codes when signing in by phone.
  • To protect the service from spam, fraud, and abuse (for example using Google reCAPTCHA Enterprise).

3. Who we share information with

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only with the following service providers, who process it on our behalf:

  • Supabase — database, authentication, and file storage.
  • Google — optional Google sign-in and reCAPTCHA Enterprise for abuse prevention.
  • Apple — optional Sign in with Apple.
  • Square — payment processing for the in-app shop. Payment card details are entered directly with Square and are never stored by us.

4. How we store and protect your information

All data is stored in Supabase with row-level security policies that restrict access to your own data and content you have permission to view. Data is transmitted using HTTPS/TLS encryption.

5. Your rights

  • Access and edit — you can view and edit your profile, posts, comments, and events from within the app at any time.
  • Delete your account — open the app, go to Settings → Account → Delete account. This permanently deletes your account and all associated content from our database. You may also email us to request deletion.
  • Withdraw consent — you can revoke permission for location, photos, camera, and notifications at any time in your device's iOS Settings.

6. Children

Worship and Yapps is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect the most recent revision.

8. Contact

For any privacy questions, requests, or account deletion help, email us at rootality@gmail.com.
