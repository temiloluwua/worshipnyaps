Worship & Yapps is a community platform for the Calgary Bible Study community. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data with respect and transparency.

1 Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and a password hash. You may optionally provide a profile photo, bio, and spiritual gifts.

Content You Create: Discussion topics, comments, event posts, direct messages, and community posts you submit are stored on our servers.

Usage Data: We collect information about how you interact with the app — pages viewed, topics opened, events RSVP'd, and similar activity — to improve the experience.

Device Information: When you access the app, we may log your device type, operating system, browser, and IP address for security and diagnostic purposes.

Communications: If you contact us for support, we retain those communications to resolve your request.

2 How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and maintain your account
  • Provide the discussion, event, messaging, and community features
  • Send notifications about activity relevant to you (replies, RSVPs, connection requests)
  • Improve, debug, and develop new features
  • Ensure the safety and integrity of our platform
  • Respond to your support requests

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising profiling.

3 Information Sharing

With Other Users: Your display name, profile photo, and public content (discussion posts, event RSVPs) are visible to other app users. Direct messages are visible only to the conversation participants.

Service Providers: We use Supabase for database hosting and authentication, and Stripe for payment processing. These providers process data only on our behalf under strict data agreements.

Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of our users and community.

4 Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on Supabase infrastructure hosted in secure cloud environments. We implement row-level security policies, encrypted connections (TLS), and hashed password storage.

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We take commercially reasonable measures to protect your information but cannot guarantee absolute security.

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data is removed within 30 days, except where we are required by law to retain it.

5 Your Rights & Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information via your profile settings
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your data upon request
  • Opt out of non-essential notifications in your settings

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address below. We will respond within 30 days.

6 Children's Privacy

Worship & Yapps is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it promptly.

7 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
