Common questions

How do I delete my account?

Open the app and go to Settings → Account → Delete account. Type DELETE to confirm, then tap Delete forever. This permanently removes your account, profile, posts, comments, events, messages, and connections. The action cannot be undone.

If you cannot access the app, email us at rootality@gmail.com from the address you signed up with and we will delete your account for you.

I can't sign in.

  • Make sure you are using the same sign-in method (email, Google, Apple, or phone) you used when creating the account.
  • Phone sign-in requires you to allow notifications to receive the verification code.
  • If you signed up with email, use the "Forgot password" link to reset it.
  • If you're still stuck, email rootality@gmail.com with the email address tied to your account.

How do I host an event?

Open the Locations tab → tap the + button → fill in the event details (title, location, date, capacity, and visibility). Public events appear on the map for everyone, friends-only events only show to your connections, and private events require an invite code.

How do I report inappropriate content or a user?

Tap the three-dot menu on any post, comment, or profile and select Report. You can also email us directly at rootality@gmail.com and we will review and take action within 24 hours.

I'm not in Calgary. Can I still use the app?

Yes — anyone can sign up and join discussions. Event discovery and map features work best in Calgary today, but we're expanding to new cities. Tell us where you'd like to see Worship and Yapps next.
