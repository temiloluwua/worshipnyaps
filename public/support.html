Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions.

Account & Sign In
How do I create an account?

Tap Sign Up on the welcome screen and enter your name, email address, and a password. After submitting, you'll be signed in immediately — no email confirmation required.

I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

On the login screen, tap Forgot password? and enter your email address. You'll receive a reset link within a few minutes. Check your spam folder if you don't see it.

If you still can't access your account, email us at worshipnyaps@gmail.com.

How do I update my profile or change my display name?

Tap your avatar in the top-right corner to open your profile, then tap Edit Profile. You can update your name, bio, profile photo, and spiritual gifts from there.

How do I delete my account?

Email us at worshipnyaps@gmail.com with the subject line "Delete my account" from the email address associated with your account. We will permanently delete your data within 30 days.

Topics & Community
How do discussion topics work?

The Topics tab shows curated Bible study discussion cards. Each card has a question or passage to reflect on. You can like, comment, bookmark, and share topics.

The Community tab shows posts created by members — prayer points, testimonies, Bible study notes, and general posts.

How do I submit a topic idea?

On the Topics tab, tap the lightbulb icon in the top-right to open the Request a Topic form. Our admins review requests and publish approved topics to the feed.

Can I edit or delete my post?

Yes. Open the post and tap the options menu (three dots or edit icon). You can edit or delete any post you authored. Admins can edit or remove any post.

Events & Locations
How do I RSVP for an event?

Open the Locations tab and tap on any event to view its details. Tap RSVP to register your attendance. You'll see the event added to your calendar and receive notifications from the organizer.

How do I host an event?

On the Locations tab, tap Host an Event. Fill in the event name, date, location, capacity, and visibility settings (public, friends only, or private). Private events require an invite code for attendees.

What is the event group chat?

When you RSVP for an event, you're added to that event's group chat. Organizers can post announcements, share directions, and answer questions. All RSVPd attendees can see and participate in the chat.

Messaging & Connections
How do I send a direct message?

Open the Messages tab and tap the compose icon. Search for the person by name, then start typing. You can also tap on any user's profile and select Message.

How do I connect with someone?

Open their profile and tap Connect. They'll receive a connection request notification. Once they accept, you'll appear in each other's connections and see friends-only content.

Shop & Orders
What payment methods are accepted?

We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) via Stripe's secure checkout. Payment details are never stored on our servers.

I have a question about my order. Who do I contact?

Email us at worshipnyaps@gmail.com with your order number and question. We'll respond within 1–2 business days.

Technical Issues
The app isn't loading or something looks broken.

Try these steps in order:

1. Refresh the page or force-close and reopen the app.
2. Clear your browser cache or app data.
3. Check your internet connection.
4. If the issue persists, email worshipnyaps@gmail.com with a description of what happened and your device/browser details.

I'm not receiving notifications.

Check your notification settings in the app (bell icon on the home screen). On iOS, make sure Worship & Yapps has permission to send notifications in Settings → Notifications. You may also need to re-enable notifications if you declined them when first installing the app.
