3Community Guidelines (Code of Conduct)

Worship N Yaps exists to help Christians find each other, study scripture together, and host real-life events. We want every interaction to reflect love, respect, and dignity. By using the app, you agree to follow these guidelines on every surface — topics, comments, messages, events, profiles, group chats, and anywhere else you post or interact.

Be kind. Be respectful. Be honest.

  • Treat others how you'd want to be treated. Disagree without attacking.
  • Speak truthfully. Don't mislead, deceive, or impersonate others.
  • Welcome new members and lift each other up.

Strictly prohibited content and behavior

The following are not allowed anywhere on the platform and will result in content removal, account suspension, or permanent ban:

  • Harassment, bullying, or threats directed at any person or group.
  • Hate speech — content that attacks or dehumanizes people based on race, ethnicity, national origin, religion, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, or any other identifying trait.
  • Sexual or pornographic material of any kind, including nudity, sexually suggestive content, or content sexualizing minors. We have zero tolerance for child sexual abuse material — confirmed reports are forwarded to authorities.
  • Violence, glorification of violence, or threats of violence against any person, group, or place of worship.
  • Self-harm or suicide content that promotes, encourages, or provides instructions for harm. (See the safety resources at the bottom of this page if you or someone you know is in crisis.)
  • Illegal activity — including the sale or promotion of drugs, weapons, stolen goods, counterfeit products, or any other illegal acts.
  • Doxing and unauthorized disclosure of personal information — sharing someone's address, phone number, workplace, or other private details without their permission.
  • Scams, fraud, spam, and unsolicited promotion — including pyramid schemes, fake giveaways, mass-DMing, fake fundraisers, or repetitive low-quality posting.
  • Predatory behavior — particularly toward minors, vulnerable adults, or new community members. This includes grooming, coercion, and any attempt to isolate someone from their support network.
  • Impersonation — pretending to be someone else (real or fictional), including pastors, leaders, or other members.
  • Content that exploits the platform's purpose — using events, chats, or topics to recruit for cults, MLMs, political campaigns, or non-faith-based commercial purposes.
  • Malware, phishing, or attempts to compromise the platform or other users' accounts.

About faith and disagreement

Christians don't all worship in the same way or believe the same things on every issue. Honest theological discussion is welcome and encouraged. Personal attacks, contempt, and weaponizing scripture to demean others are not.

5Reports, moderation & enforcement

Every post, comment, message, event, and profile has a "Report" option. If you see content that violates these guidelines, please report it. Our team reviews reports promptly and takes action including:

  • Removing the offending content
  • Warning the user
  • Suspending the user's account temporarily
  • Permanently banning the user from the platform
  • Notifying authorities in cases involving illegal activity or imminent harm

You can also block any user from Settings or their profile. Blocking prevents them from contacting you, seeing your posts, or appearing in your event attendee lists.

To report abuse or escalate a serious concern, email support@worshipandyapps.com with the username and a description of what happened. We aim to respond within 24 hours for safety issues and 1-2 business days for everything else.

6Your safety at in-person events

Worship N Yaps helps you find and host gatherings in the real world. We can't verify every host, attendee, or location, so please use good judgment:

  • Meet in public or well-attended places when meeting new people.
  • Bring a friend if you can. Tell someone where you're going.
  • Trust your instincts. Leave any situation that feels unsafe.
  • If a host or attendee makes you uncomfortable, block them, report them, and contact local authorities if you feel unsafe.

Worship N Yaps is not responsible for the conduct of users or third parties at any in-person event. Hosts and attendees participate at their own risk.
